Bitcoin

what is bitcoin?

Today

Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency created in 2009 that uses cryptography to secure and verify transactions. It has a limited supply of 21 million units, making it deflationary. It can be bought and sold on online exchanges and used to purchase goods and services. Bitcoin's innovative technology has potential to disrupt traditional finance, but its volatility and lack of regulation raise concerns about its stability. Continue reading

About Cryptos

May 3, 2023 by Eloy Mark

A cryptocurrency, crypto-currency, or crypto is a digital currency designed to work as a medium of exchange through a computer network that is not reliant on any central authority, such as a government or bank, to uphold or maintain it.[2] It is a decentralized system for verifying that the parties to a transaction have the money they claim to have, eliminating the need for traditional intermediaries, such as banks, when funds are being transferred between two entities
